Pre-loaded: 2026 Texas QAP

The Qualified Allocation Plan governs how Texas awards Housing Tax Credits — the primary financing tool for affordable housing development. It controls site eligibility, scoring criteria, disqualifiers, underwriting rules, and application requirements.

  • Site scoring: proximity to services, income targeting, green building, community support
  • Disqualifiers: flood plains, pipeline proximity, missing reports, de-concentration failures
  • Underwriting: financial feasibility, market analysis, appraisal standards
  • Timeline: pre-application through carryover agreement deadlines
